Welcome

Diggidy’s ® home of the “Frozen Treat”™ and “Frozen Dessert” ™ was born in concept in early 2012 amidst the frozen yogurt boom. After visiting many frozen yogurt shops, several things became clear. We created a “Better Frozen Treat” ™. We knew we could do better creating a “Frozen Dessert Factory” ™, not just a yogurt shop. Why just yogurt? Where’s the ice cream? What about custard? Custard is still cool right? 30 toppings... why stop there Diggidy’s is the "Ultimate Frozen Treat” ™ destination. Every shop we visited was almost the same yet all claimed to be different or better.  Diggidy’s “Frozen Treat Factory” ™ was born

We wanted our concept to change the industry and reinvent the “Frozen Treat Destination” ™. That’s why we are not a froyo shop but rather a “Frozen Desert Destination” ™. We broke out of that mold to provide a better way cool concept that has it all. Ice cream, smoothies, yogurt, hand dipped, gelato, custard, candy, and over 250 toppings. You can also make your own floats and a Bomb Diggidy’s ™... our way cool blended frozen dessert. And unlike the other ice cream places where you get one or two sweet types in your blended cup... you can pick from over 250 and mix in as many as you want. At Diggidy’s we also offer our delicious “Frozen Delight” ™, “Frozen Smoothie” ™, and “Smoothie Float” ™. At Diggidy's we pride ourselves on breaking the mold and making the ultimate “Frozen Treat Experience” ™.
