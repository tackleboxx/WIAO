Welcome

Diggidys was born in concept in early 2012 amidst the frozen yogurt boom. After visiting many frozen yogurt shops several things became clear. We knew we could do better. Why just yogurt? Where’s the ice cream? What about custard? Custard is still cool right? 30 toppings... why stop there. Every shop we visited was almost the same yet all claimed to be different or better.

We wanted our concept to change the industry and reinvent the frozen dessert. That’s why we are not a froyo shop. Every store is named Yo this and Yo that. We broke out of that mold to provide a better way cool concept that has it all. Ice cream, smoothies, yogurt, hand dipped, gelato, custard, candy, and over 250 toppings. You can also make your own floats, and bomb Diggidys... our way cool blended frozen dessert. And unlike the other ice cream place where you get one or two sweet types in your blended cup... you can pick from over 200 and mix in as many as you want. At Diggidy's we pride ourselves on breaking the mold and making the greatest Frozen Treat Factory experience.

Franchise - Whoaaa.... hold on a second. Sure we broke the mold and completely reinvented the frozen dessert, but give us a few weeks to work the final details out.

Locations - Anderson, IN. 2 more locations coming soon.
